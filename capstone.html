What are Table Topics?

Table topics are a chance to practice impromptu speaking.

The Purpose

To help practice organizing our thoughts on the fly. It's a time-tested practice for rising to the occasion in interviews, dates, meetings, and more.

The Goal

Speak to the Green - that's 1 minute

Wrap it up by the Red - that's 2 minutes

If you don't finish by the Black (2:30), you're disqualified

How it works

  • Search for a word or short phrase
  • The Table Topics Picture Generator will find 5 words related to your search and 3 images for each word
  • Choose any image or two that spark an idea
  • Hit the start button
  • Start talking about whatever comes to mind

Speaking Tips, Tricks, and Techniques

  • Relax and have fun
  • Just run with whatever subject comes to mind
  • If practicing with others, be respectful, but try not to censor yourself
  • Tell a story about yourself or something you've just heard
  • Feel free to just make things up. This is performance practice; what you say doesn't have to be true
  • The key is humor and wit is to break expectations (don't just say what your audience expects to hear)
